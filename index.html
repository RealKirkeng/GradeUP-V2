Frequently Asked Questions

What makes GradeUp tutors different?

All our tutors are verified university students with proven academic credentials. We check transcripts and diplomas to ensure quality education, unlike open marketplaces. You're learning from peers who recently mastered the same subjects.

Why is the first lesson free?

We believe in building trust from day one. Your free first lesson lets you experience our tutor's teaching style before committing financially. We want you to find the perfect match!

Are there discounts for students?

Absolutely! Verify your student email address and get 15% off all subscription packages. We also offer group lesson discounts and special rates for schools and universities.

Can I book online or in-person?

Both! Choose what works for you. Our platform supports flexible scheduling with online sessions via video chat or in-person tutoring depending on tutor availability and location.

What subjects does GradeUp cover?

We specialize in high school and university courses including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Economics, Languages, Literature, Computer Science, and more. All curriculum-aligned subjects.

How does GradeUp support the UN Sustainable Development Goals?

We're committed to SDG 4 (Quality Education) by making high-quality tutoring affordable, and SDG 8 (Decent Work) by creating flexible income opportunities for university students. No hidden fees, just transparent support.
